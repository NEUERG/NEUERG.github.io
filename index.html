    Who We are

About Us

Big Data in Business is a new organization on campus that recognizes that big data is a valuable resource for companies and organizations throughout the world. We want to foster a platform where students from all disciplines can come and learn more about the field and how it can benefit both them and their career. We welcome people of all levels of experience, and don't require or expect anyone to be proficient in key data science technologies to attend a meeting. We simply hope to teach members the value of Big Data, and some core technical skills needed to take advantage of it.
